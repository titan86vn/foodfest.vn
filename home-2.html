Food Tour

Food Tour



The Exploration of World Culinary – ONLY in FOODFEST 2017. You will obtain precious opportunities to taste various types of food and drink from over 300 globally reputable brands in F&B industry.

o Taste of Vietnam: Vietnamese cuisine is recognized to stay in the top 17 countries of the world. All the visitors will definitely find outstanding experiences of Vietnamese cuisine from the North to the South.

o Taste of Europe: A signature Food Tour to European countries with more than 200 favorite food and drink brands.

o Taste of Asia: Asian Food is considered as the world’s treasure, which significantly suits esteemed visitors that are passionate about culinary delights. Amazingly, Food Fest 2017 will publish this treasure to the world or to multifaceted audience.

Master Chef Show: Jack Lee

Master Chef Show: Jack Lee



Jack Lee – an American Vietnamese Chef, known as a celebrity in culinary arts.

After finishing culinary degree in California and Le Cordon Bleu shows, he has practiced his skills in Michelin 3-star restaurants in many countries. Jack Lee used to cook for world – class celebrities known as Angelina Jolie, Clint Eastwood and Hilary Swank.

Jack Lee’s cooking performances in FOODFEST 2017 promise to bring unforgettable moments to all the food lovers.
